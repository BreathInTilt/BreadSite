Meet the Bakers

At Hleb Planete, we believe that the best bread is made by hand. That's why we do everything the old-fashioned way. We use only the finest ingredients, and we never rush the process. Our bread is made with love, and it shows in every bite.

Our Philosophy

We've been making bread for over 30 years, and we still get up at 3am every day to make sure our customers have the freshest, most delicious bread possible. We don't cut corners, and we never compromise on quality. That's the Hleb Planete difference.

Our Bakery

Our bakery is located in the heart of the city, and it's where all the magic happens. Our bakers arrive before dawn to start the ovens, and they work tirelessly to create the perfect loaves of bread.

Our Team

Our team is a tight-knit group of passionate food lovers. We're like family, and we treat our customers the same way. We take pride in our work, and we're always striving to improve.

The Breadmaking Process

Breadmaking is an art, and we take it very seriously. We use traditional techniques and time-honored recipes to create bread that's rich in flavor and texture. It's a labor of love, but we wouldn't have it any other way.
